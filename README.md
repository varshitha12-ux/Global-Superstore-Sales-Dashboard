## **📊Global Superstore Sales Dashboard|Power BI**



#### 

#### **📌Overview**



The Global Superstore Sales Dashboard is an interactive Business Intelligence solution developed using Microsoft Power BI. The dashboard analyzes sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, product performance, category trends, and regional performance using the Global Superstore dataset (2011–2014).



The objective of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations and KPIs, enabling data-driven decision-making.





#### **🎯Business Problem**



A retail company operating across multiple regions needs a centralized dashboard to monitor business performance and answer key business questions such as:



\- How have sales, profit, and orders changed over time?

\- Which product categories are the most profitable?

\- Which regions generate the highest sales and profit?

\- Who are the top-performing customers?

\- Which products contribute the most to revenue and profit?

\- Which customer segments drive business growth?

##### **💡Business Insights**



\- Sales increased consistently from 2011 to 2014.

\- Technology was the highest-performing category in both sales and profit.

\- The Central region generated the highest sales and profit.

\- Consumer customers contributed the largest share of sales and profit.

\- High-selling products were not always the most profitable.

\- Top customers by sales differed from top customers by profit, highlighting the importance of profitability analysis.
